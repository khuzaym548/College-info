About us

Superhouse Group is a multi-unit and multi-product conglomerate with brand leadership in Leather, Leather goods, Footwear manufacturing and exports. The Group is well equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and a skilled workforce to produce a diverse range and quality of Leather, leather goods and textile garments, appreciated globally.

Global Footprint

A US $200 million Superhouse Group has 22 Units, employing a workforce of 8000+ skilled personnel and more than 50 Countries. Our ISO 9002 certification reaffirms our commitment to quality. Stringent EN 345-norms makes us one of the most respected manufacturers amongst the European Importer/buyer fraternity. Equipped with requisite infrastructure and compliance to high-quality standards, we easily conform to CSA, ANZ & SABS Standards. A perfect blend of skilled workforce and competent managers has helped us in carving out an enviable position for ourselves in the global market, amongst contemporaries.

Men's Footwear

We are one of the largest manufacturers and exporters of men’s footwear from India . Our clients are located in countries such as France, UK, Germany, USA, Canada, Spain, Scandinavia, the Netherlands, etc. We have a capacity to produce 6000 pairs per day of cemented shoes in our 2 men’s footwear manufacturing units located at Unnao (near Kanpur). In addition, we also have 32 stations direct moulding PU machine from Italy with a capacity to produce 1,000 pairs per day shoes and sandals. We have a team of highly qualified designers in our R& D center in Unnao. They develop samples based on the design inputs from our experienced team of designers based in Europe.
