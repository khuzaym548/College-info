●→ Guest Lecture ISRO

Continuing with the commitment to provide learning opportunities to the skillful engineers of tomorrow, the EC department of Allenhouse Institute of Technology organized a seminar on 25 November, 2022 for B.Tech students. The expert for the same was Mr. Manish Kumar Singh, Director of the Ministry of Railways (IES). He suggested many tips to crack government jobs for engineers (like IES, ISRO, DRDO, GATE, etc.). He is also a good motivational speaker and meditational expert. He shared his valuable experience related to meditation like the DELTA wave and many more. This seminar was very valuable for students. It was a interactive session wherein students raised several queries and each one of them was answered to their satisfaction. This seminar serves as an enlightening platform where experts from ISRO, renowned scientists, and esteemed professionals convene to discuss groundbreaking developments, innovations, and achievements in space research and exploration. Mr. Manish Kumar Singh, with his expertise and leadership in the field of railway engineering services, adds a unique perspective by bridging the realms of railway technology and space science.

●→ Oorja Sports Event

“There may be people that have more talent than you, but there’s no excuse for anyone to work harder than you.”

-Derek Jeter, American Baseball Player

Oorja- The sports event of Allenhouse Group of Institutions was successfully conducted on 28th and 29th of April 2023. The two-day sports event has a total of six different games like Badminton (both singles and doubles), Table tennis (both singles and doubles), Volleyball, Chess, Carrom and Arm Wrestling. In all these events, both girls and boys participated with full enthusiasm and had a lot of fun. All the participants (including the Winners and Runner-ups) were praised and awarded. Participants showcased their skills and athleticism in Badminton (both singles and doubles), Table Tennis (both singles and doubles), Volleyball, Chess, Carrom, and the adrenaline-pumping contest of Arm Wrestling. The diverse range of games ensured that there was something for everyone, catering to different interests and abilities.
